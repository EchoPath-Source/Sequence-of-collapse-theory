## Working title

**Sequence of Collapse Theory: A Research Framework for Collapse, Memory, Observation, and Cosmological Structure**

## Abstract placeholder

The Sequence of Collapse Theory (SoCT) proposes a layered framework in which reality resolves through interaction, observation, and persistent memory. This synthesis paper connects four research tracks: memory-field gravity and SPARC validation (P1), Planck nucleation and two-timescale dark-energy phenomenology (P2), causal inversion and directional cosmological memory (P3), and observer-dependent decoherence experiments (P4). The goal is not to present SoCT as confirmed, but to translate its source concepts into operational claims, shared equations, falsification criteria, and staged empirical programs.

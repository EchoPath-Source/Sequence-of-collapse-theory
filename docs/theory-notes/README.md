# Theory Notes

This folder holds speculative and conceptual extensions to the Sequence of Collapse Theory.

These notes are **not** peer-reviewed claims and should not be treated as validated physics. They are research scaffolds: places to preserve hypotheses, clarify terminology, identify what would have to be true, and separate conceptual exploration from reproducible experiments, datasets, and manuscript-grade papers.

## Current notes

- `origin-point-postulate.md` — frames dimension zero as total dimensional potential in unexpressed form.
- `origin-curvature-synthesis.md` — synthesizes the Origin Point Postulate with Q-RRG: dimensions as stabilized curvature-expressions of the Origin singularity.
- `quark-projection-hypothesis.md` — proposes that quark properties may be 3+1D readouts of deeper hidden-dimensional projection structure.
- `dimensional-bridge-principle.md` — generalizes the hidden-degree pattern across scale transitions from Planck cells to cosmic structure.
- `planck-cell-unfolding-hypothesis.md` — frames the Big Bang as the unfolding/nucleation of a completed compactified Planck-cell-like state.
- `threshold-unfolding-principle.md` — formalizes the idea that compression beyond closure capacity opens a new accessible direction.

## Handling policy

1. Label speculative status clearly.
2. Preserve conceptual provenance.
3. Do not mix these notes with empirical results unless a testable prediction is explicitly identified.
4. Treat these notes as hypothesis generators for later mathematical modeling, simulations, or papers.

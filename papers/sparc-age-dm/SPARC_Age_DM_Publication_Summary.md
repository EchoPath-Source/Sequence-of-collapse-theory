Status: PRELIMINARY / requires independent audit.

This document records the uploaded SPARC Age-DM publication summary artifact. It should be treated as a research note, not as final proof of a mechanism.

- Pearson age vs outer f_DM: r = +0.201, p = 0.0077, n = 175.
- Spearman age vs outer f_DM: rho = +0.211, p = 0.0050.
- Mid-mass reported signal: r = +0.456, p = 0.0003.

## Falsification / Weakening Criteria

CIH is weakened if:

- predicted axes do not match observed candidate anomalies;
- predicted amplitude is excluded by isotropy constraints;
- polarization signatures are absent;
- anomalies are fully explained by systematics or kinematic effects;
- the model lacks a quantitative prediction before comparing to data.

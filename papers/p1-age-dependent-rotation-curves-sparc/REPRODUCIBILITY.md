## Claim boundary

Safe current claim:

> P1 explores whether age-dependent structure in galaxy rotation-curve data can be organized with a memory-field-inspired effective-gravity scaffold.

Do not claim yet:

- SPARC proves SoCT,
- dark matter has been replaced,
- a physical memory field has been detected,
- the effect is publication-grade before reproducibility packet completion.

## Product boundary

P1 may inspire product-neutral terms such as memory-field-inspired modeling, adaptive memory, or field-inspired dynamics. It does not certify EchoPath, EchoGenesis, Witness-Q, or any product claim.

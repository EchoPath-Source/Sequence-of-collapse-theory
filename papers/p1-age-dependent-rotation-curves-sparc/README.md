## Purpose

This is the canonical P1 folder from the March 2026 SOC Publication Roadmap.

P1 is the conservative entry point into peer-reviewed literature. It focuses on the claim that galaxies of similar baryonic mass but different formation epochs show systematically different kinematic profiles, consistent with collapse-history dependent gravitational behavior.

## Core claim

> Galaxies of similar baryonic mass but different formation epochs show systematically different kinematic profiles, consistent with collapse-history dependent gravitational behavior.

## Key roadmap result

The roadmap records that **Prediction 3 is confirmed from the SPARC database**: same mass, different epoch, different kinematics.
